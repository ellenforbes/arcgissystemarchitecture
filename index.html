Pembroke's ESRI Package

ArcGIS Online is Esri-hosted cloud GIS with minimal IT overhead. ArcGIS Enterprise is a self-hosted platform giving your organisation full control over infrastructure, security, and data. Pembroke currently use ArcGIS Online, at approximately AUD $12,000 / year in licence costs.

Enterprise licence costs have decreased over the last decade, though Esri don't publish formal pricing — an accurate figure requires advice from our rep. Depending on infrastructure, costs could range from $50,000 to $100,000 per year.

If Pembroke had strong existing IT infrastructure, Enterprise would be worth formally investigating. However, given our current IT maturity, ArcGIS Online remains the appropriate choice.
